Our Story

We are small local skateboard company who specialize in making boards for riders who believe in the dream of skateboarding. The idea for the board42 came to the team while brainstorming how to shoot and create a video documenting the a "natural elments" that a skateboarder has with his board.

Designed in San Francisco by three guys (Dave, Mike and Saen) who have a passion and incredible stamina for skateboarding. Our boards are designed out of recycled old skateboards, and handcrafted to best suit each buyers needs. This makes each one, one of a kind. We wanted to create a board that has already been broken in by the previos users of the combined boards to create that natural feel. We dedicate our time to make sure our products are unqiue by getting local artists to be featured and create a board for us. We want to bring together the local art and skateboarding scene to create a community and encourage more collaboration.

Designed for skaters who believe in the natural feel of a broken in board. Our product is designed, built and quality tested to be best suited for a true skaters needs.

Saen

"True sunglasses at night guy"

Sean is that guy who is awlays wearing the latest local clothing to help promote new startups. He has the ability to skate anything and come out smoothly. Just stick a board in his hand and he is gone. That's just the mystery..., until we catch it on tape.

Mike

"Water cooler socializer"

Mike is a dedicated skate instructor and father. He loves teaching the youth and kids new skateboarding tricks. You can always catch him at the community skatepark surrounded by kids. He has an amazing style and approach to skateboarding that is not to be missed.

Dave

"Never passes on a good donut"

Dave is our all around go to guy for positive skateboard movements to change the world. You can always catch him chatting up the locals about how to make a difference in the world with the help of skateboarding. His passion radiates through his style and onto the street when he boards. His every move is golden.
